Childhood Years

     During my childhood, I used to quite the troublemaker1. Like other children my age, I was very carefree2. I spent most of my time outside the my house and I even played with my neighbors and friends3. I got into all manners of mischief4. There are even times when I quarreled with my sibling5. Both of my parents worked in order to maked ends meet6. My father worked in the Developement Bank of the Philippines in Makati City7. My mother, on the other hand, worked in the University of the East in Metro Manila8. Since our house was built in Antipolo City, we often stayed at my grandmother's house in San Juan Citywhenever I needed to attend school in St. Johns Academy9. We only returned during the weekends10.

     Living in the house, along with my grandmother, were my other relatives11. They worked together when tackling problems12. They took care of me while my parents were busy at work13. I had a lot of people who took care of me so I never felt lonely14. It wouldn't even be too far to say that I spent most of my childhood living with my grandmother and other relatives15. As a child things always looked bright16. The simplest of family gatherings were always enough to excite me17. Learning new things was also a unique experience 18. Even having dreams seemed simple yet exciting19. I once even dreamed of becoming a pilot and travelling the world20.

Teenage Years

     When I reached my teenage years, I realized a lot of things1. I realized that things are not always as simple as they seem2. I learned that in order to become a functioning member of society, I needed to gain more knowledge and experiences3. I also learned that in order to achieve my dreams, I needed to work hard for it 4. Which is why I decided to try harder5. I continued to study in St. John's Academy, a private school, in San Juan City6. I increasingly became very busy with my studies7.

     The free time that I once spent relaxing leisurely needed to be spent on studies as well as maintaining my grades instead8. In order to further improve my academical activity, I decided to aim for the University of the East for college9. I needed to learn the proper knowledge that I required in order to prepare for the future 10. On top of that I also had to balance my schedule between various factors11. Two of these factors are resting and studying12. Needing to balance my lifestyle while encountering various obstacles physically, mentally and emotionally was not easy13. But through facing all this hardships, I was able to slowly mature14. I became more aware of my surroundings15. I even started to observe others more than interacting with them 16. I also became less lively and talkative17. I did this in order to improve my manners in preparation for adulthood18. I became increasingly determined to improve19. Through every obstacle I faced I learned that a person can't achieve any progress without putting in any serious effort20.

College Years

     When I became a college student, I've already transferred to the University of the East in Manila1. But things didn't exactly get any easier2. I was expecting college to be similar but slightly easier than gradeschool3. This was because most of my experiences during my teenage years were had already prepared me for it4. But now I realized how different things actually are5. College turns out to be a whole new experience entirely6. This became even more so when the Corona Virus appeared7. Due to its occurrence, what everyone once considered as irregular became the new norm8. Quarantines were implemented and face-to-face classes were indefinitely suspended9. In order to adapt to the situation, the majority of educational institutions as well as various facilities resorted to online methods10. Various businesses and institutions that couldn't adapt to online services even closed down11. Wearing facemasks outside even became required as well12.

     Various changes occurred that changed people perception13. But despite all these changes for everyone, nothing really changed much in my lifestyle personally14. I'm not really as outgoing as others which allowed me to endure staying indoors for longer periods of time15. I simply continued striving for improvement without too much issues16.Now that I've finally reached this far, I now need to focus more on the bigger picture17. This is the part of my life where I need to make big decisions which would impact my future and I can't be hindered by all these changes18. I have to decide on my course based on my desired future occupation19. This is the part of my life where I decide my future20.
