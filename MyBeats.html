Jazz

One of my favorite genres of music is Jazz. It's a genre of music that I find very soothing to listen to. I am very fond of the lively yet relaxed feeling it gives off as well as the retro image it creates. I was first introduced to this type of music when I first visited a cafe and I've enjoyed listening to it ever since. Listening to it helps relieve my stress and fatigue as well as serves as a way of spending my time leisurely.

Classical

Another one of my favorite genres of music is Classical music. I am very fond of classical settings and this particular genre of music is the very manifestation of the so-called "Classical Era". This type of music exudes an aura of formality and extravagance that allows me to experience all manner of emotions each time I listen to it. With only a slight change in pace, it can alter the mood of a person significantly. I first came accross this genre of music when me and my family went to the Mall of Asia and a public piano performance was being held in the middle of the square. Some may say that Classical music is "Old and Outdated" but to me, nothing beats the classics.

Country Music

My third favorite genre of music is Country music. This music genre evokes various memories from my childhood. It sometimes carries the slight melacholic tone of a person who misses his place of birth. To me, this genre of music, whether it's a country music from my country or a foreign one, still helps me recall distant memories from my childhood and brings about a sense of nostalgia. The very first country music I heard was "Take Me Home, Country Roads" by John Denver and I've loved this genre ever since.
