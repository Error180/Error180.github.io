Coron

Coron, Palawan is one of the places that I plan to visit in the near future1. Coron is a 1st class municipality in the province of Palawan, here in the Philippines2. It has caught the attention of many, supposedly, due to its World War II wreck diving spots, beautiful rock formations, and unbelievably clear waters, including me3. It is also considered the more quiet and laidback sibling of El Nido, another famous Palawan destination because of its gorgeous waters and scenery4.

Since I am a big fan of beautiful sceneries and places with a peaceful atmosphere, this location can serve as a place for me to relax and unwind along with my family5.
