Boracay

Boracay is a small island in the central Philippines1. It's quite well known for its resorts and beaches2. Along the west coast, the White Beach is backed by palm trees, bars and restaurants3. Offshore, coral reefs and shipwrecks are home to quite diverse species' of marine life4.

I've already been to this place once before but that was quite a long time ago which is why I want to revisit this scenic location in order to reminisce about the past as well as take a breather from the stress of everyday life5.
