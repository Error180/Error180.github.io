Matterhorn

Matterhorn is a mountain of the Alps1. It straddles the main watershed and border between Switzerland and Italy2. It is a large, near-symmetric pyramidal peak in the extended Monte Rosa area of the Pennine Alps3. Its summit is 4,478 metres high, making it one of the highest summits in the Alps and Europe4.

My reason for wanting to visit this particular location, in Switzerland, is simply to take in the beautiful sites and unwind with the peaceful atmosphere which I am quite fond of5.
